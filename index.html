Hi there.

I'm Mars. For the past 7 years, I've lead teams, projects, products and processes from scratch. I've been an Operations Manager, Project Manager, Product Manager, Account Manager and co-founder. All while working on personal projects where I had to be a programmer, property consultant, recruiter, financial advisor, digital marketer, hiker, technical writer and a student, among others.

A lot of times, I bit more than I can chew - such as when I went to a 6 month coding bootcamp while managing 50 people at work, or when I ran a marketing campaign and had to respond to 100 warm leads a day on my personal Facebook (I later built a bot to handle this). But that's okay, in fact it's been by design. I approached my early 20s with a heart for heavy hustling, experimenting and failing fast, all in the name of self improvement and self discovery.

Now in my mid-20s, I can say I've worn a lot of hats and scratched a lot of surfaces that I have a good idea where I want to be in the next 7 years - I want to build a career in tech, particularly in the field of data science. And I want to take it easy. More careful selection of projects, more focus on work that impacts people's lives, more bandwidth for creativeness, more listening to my body when it's tired. More time for holistic growth.
